A two-color Risograph-style illustration on textured paper. A single robot sits at a workbench with a large analog dial in front of it. The dial has three labeled notches. At the smallest notch, the needle doesn't move at all. At the middle notch, the needle swings dramatically. At the largest notch, the needle barely trembles. Behind the robot, three shadows on the wall show three different robot silhouettes — tiny, medium, and towering — each reacting differently to the same input. OPTION 3: Same signal, different reception

A two-color Risograph-style illustration on textured paper. A single megaphone or broadcast tower in the center emits concentric signal waves. Three robots at different distances receive the signal differently: a small robot on the left lets the waves pass right through it, unaffected. A medium robot in the center absorbs the waves visibly — its posture shifts, its eyes widen, its hands move to a defensive position. A large robot on the right stands still, but the waves enter its body and rearrange its internal gears and wiring without changing its outward expression. Deep teal and muted red on textured cream paper. No text or logos.

OPTION 4: Caveman interaction

Behind it, a caveman figure approaches with a large stone club, and where the club strikes the shields they crumble and dissolve into simple geometric shapes. The robot's defensive armor is half-intact and half-stripped away, revealing a simpler, leaner robot underneath. The contrast: elaborate protection on one side, stripped-down efficiency on the other. Deep teal and muted red on textured cream paper. No text or logos.

OPTION 5: Caveman meets paranoid robot

A two-color Risograph-style illustration on textured paper. Split composition. On the left, a detailed robot frantically building walls, adding locks, installing cameras, writing warning signs — covered in defensive infrastructure. On the right, the same robot after a caveman has visited: walls reduced to a single fence, cameras gone, just the essential structure remaining. A small caveman figure sits on top of the simplified structure, satisfied. The robot looks confused but lighter. Deep teal and pale rose on textured cream paper. No text or logos.

OPTION 6: Thinking depth / amplification

A two-color Risograph-style illustration on textured paper, featuring a vertical triptych. Three panels show the same robot thinking, with thought bubbles of increasing size. In the first panel (low effort), a small thought bubble contains a simple, calm scene. In the second panel (high effort), a larger thought bubble shows the same scene but with more defensive elements — walls, locks, warning signs appearing. In the third panel (max effort), a massive thought bubble overflows with fortifications, guard towers, and surveillance cameras, while the robot itself looks increasingly vigilant. Deep teal and pale rose on textured cream paper. Detailed and intricate mechanical style. No text or logos.
